How to Update?

To update DivineBeats follow the steps: 1. Flash the DivineBeats Remover to remove previous version of DivineBeats. 2. In Recovery [eg. TWRP] clear system only. 3. Dirty Flash your current ROM. [If you don't know what is this GOOGLE IT.] 4. Flash the newest version of DivineBeats. 5. Clear Cache and Dalvik Cache.

Q.1: Why I have to follow so many steps? Ans: It is just to ensure that you get fully functional DivineBeats after update.

Q.2: How to remove DivineBeats completely? Ans: Just follow the first three steps above mentioned.
